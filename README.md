# 🐄 Gokula Health – Smart Livestock Management System

Gokula Health is an Android-based Smart Livestock Management System developed during the internship program at MindMatrix using Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Firebase concepts, and Generative AI assisted development workflows.

The application helps dairy farmers digitally manage livestock records, milk production, vaccination schedules, cattle health monitoring, and farmer profiles through a modern Android application interface.

---

- 🐄 Cow Registration System
- 🥛 Milk Diary Management
- 💉 Vaccination Reminder System

# GokulaHealthCare

## Problem Statement
Managing cattle healthcare and dairy-related activities manually is difficult for farmers. Important records such as milk yield, vaccination schedules, and cow registrations can be hard to maintain properly. This project provides a digital solution to manage cattle healthcare and dairy farm activities efficiently.

---

## Objectives
- To simplify cattle healthcare management
- To maintain cow registration records digitally
- To track milk yield and milk calculations
- To provide vaccination reminders and alerts
- To help farmers manage dairy activities effectively
